How to Play

  • ▲ / ▼ Pitch the nose — controls default to joystick-style (inverted): the lower arrow pulls up. Flip it on the menu.
  • Loops Hold pitch all the way around for a full loop
  • Throttle Power lever on the right sets your speed
  • 🔫 Fire your twin Vickers guns at enemy planes
  • 💣 Drop bombs on ground targets
  • Altitude Hug the ground and the camera zooms in; climb high and it zooms out
  • Keyboard ↑/↓ pitch, ←/→ throttle, Space fire, B bomb, P pause
